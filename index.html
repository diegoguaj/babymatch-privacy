♥
1

Who we are

BabyMatch is an iOS app that helps couples find baby names they both love. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information when you use it.

BabyMatch is an independent app. If you have any questions about this policy, you can reach us at the contact details at the bottom of this page.

2

What data we collect

BabyMatch currently stores the following data locally on your device only, using Apple's standard on-device storage (UserDefaults):

Your name and your partner's name — entered during onboarding, stored only on your device and used to personalise the in-app experience.
Your name preferences — which gender and style filters you've selected, your swipe history, matches, and shortlisted names.
Purchase records — which collections or features you've unlocked, stored on-device and used to restore your purchases.

None of this data is transmitted to any external server, database, or third party. It lives only on your phone.

3

What data we do not collect

We do not collect:

Your email address, phone number, or any contact information.
Location data, camera or microphone access, or any device sensors.
Analytics, usage tracking, crash reports, or advertising identifiers.

BabyMatch does not use any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking frameworks.

4

In-App Purchases

BabyMatch offers optional one-time purchases to unlock additional name collections and features. All payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment details — Apple handles the entire transaction.

Apple's privacy policy governs all payment processing. You can review it at apple.com/legal/privacy.
5

Partner pairing (coming soon)

A future update will introduce real-time partner pairing, allowing two people to sync their swipes and see mutual matches together. When this feature launches, it will require creating an account and syncing data via a secure cloud backend.

We will update this privacy policy before that feature is released, and we will be transparent about exactly what data is stored and how it is protected. Partner pairing will be entirely opt-in.

6

Children's privacy

BabyMatch is designed for use by adults — specifically, expectant parents. The app is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

7

Data retention and deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you are in full control of it at all times. To delete all app data:

Go to iPhone Settings → General → iPhone Storage → BabyMatch → Delete App. This removes the app and all associated data from your device permanently.

You can also reset your preferences from within the app: open the Profile tab and tap Reset Onboarding in the Account section.

8

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, particularly as new features (such as partner pairing) are introduced. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of BabyMatch after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
